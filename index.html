11FAQ

Questions,
answered

Do you identify individuals? +
No. All psychographics and coordination are computed at the group or cluster level — never per account, never per person.
Is this a replacement for polling? +
No. Sentra is a complement to survey research. It measures discourse as it moves, in real time, where opinion actually forms.
Which platforms do you cover? +
Public Facebook today, with Telegram next. Coverage extends as the standard expands across Eastern Europe.
Do you forecast events? +
We see the move forming. Sentra surfaces early warning from observed signals — it does not predict outcomes.
Do we need technical knowledge to start? +
No. Sentra is delivered as briefings and a dashboard. An analyst narrates the signal; the method stays auditable.
